Profit Projection for French Truck Coffee

During my time working at a coffee shop I noticed that the manager was recording all the purchasing and income data into a csv file. I asked if I could use the csv files to try and predict profits over the next year. I took the csv files and formatted all the data to be day by day, and created two regression algorithms, one to predict the sales and one to predict the cost. Then it was as simple as finding the difference between each prediction for each month and pushing it to a new csv.

Revamp of University of New Orleans Tutor Service

For a project me and a team of five decided to revamp the university of new orleans tutor service. The old service had no way of informing you of your position in the queue, or scheduling an appointment; as well as visually being bland. My position for this project was to implement the views that both the tutor has when they are doing the tutoring, the view the student has when being tutored, and the queue projection system which visually shows you your place in line.
